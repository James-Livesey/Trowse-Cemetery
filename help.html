Help

Welcome to Trowse Cemetery's plot databasing and mapping system! This system provides you with an easy way to manage plots and display them on a map. Seamlessly add and remove plots, manage occupants, and easily find anything using the search function.

Getting started

Getting started is easy on this system â”€ if you haven't already, press the Sign in button in the top-right hand corner. If you've got an account with this system, simply enter your email address and password. If you don't, press Sign up, then enter your full name, email address and password.

By default, editing for your account will be disabled until a trusted user enables editing on your account for you. You can check if you are allowed to edit the database by going to the Data tab and seeing if you can edit the database without any errors. If you can't, you'll need to contact a user who already has edit permissions to add you to the whitelist of users who are allowed to edit.

Allowing someone else to edit

If you are a user who is allowed to edit the database, sign in (if you haven't done already), then press on your name in the top-right hand corner, followed by the Settings button. This will show the settings for your account.

To allow the user to edit, find their name and email address within the User account permissions section of Settings. When you're absolutely certain that you can trust the user with editing permissions, toggle the switch to the on position by their name in the Can edit? field.

Revoking permissions

If you feel that a user cannot be trusted, you can disable their editing permission by toggling the same switch to the off position. They will no longer be able to edit the plot database, or even change permissions of other users.

Managing your account

Managing your account on this system is vital to keeping it secure and updated. To manage your account, press on your name in the top-right hand corner, followed by the Settings button. This will show the settings for your account.

To change your name shown on this account, enter it into the Full name input, then press the Save button.

To change the email address that you use to sign in with, press the Change email button, then enter your current email address and password (for security reasons), then enter your new email address and press the Save button.

To change the password that you use to sign in with, press the Change password button, then enter your current email address and password (for security reaons), then enter your new password and press the Save button.

To delete your account, press the Delete account button, then enter your current email address and password (for security reasons), then press the Delete button. This will delete your account and the data associated with it will not be recovered. Please note that the plot database will not be deleted.
